DAY 4

Live In Present

Being present. It sounds so simple, right? But too often we are taken out of the present moment. Our heads get cluttered with the mind chatter of past, broken memories, and anxieties and fears of what’s to come; all the while, we look past the beauty and simplicity of the present-this moment, right now. Here is the solution, just make a diversion from that past event by making some future goals, by spending time with closed friends and family and just take a break from your daily routine and do what you Love.
