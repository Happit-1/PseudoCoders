Time Is Everlasting, But People Aren’t. Keep In Touch With People Whom You Love.

DAY 17

Interact With Friends

There’s nothing better than catching up with someone face-to-face. But that’s not always possible. Give them a call, drop them a note or chat to them online instead. Keep the lines of communication open. It’s good for you! It is so important to keep the people who care about you around and show them that you care about them. Good friendships don’t always come easily during every stage of life. However, if you already have a life long friendship, then you’ll never have to worry about not having anyone to talk to.
