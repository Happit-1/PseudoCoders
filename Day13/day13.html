DAY 13

Set Achievable Goals.

Nothing demotivates more than not being able to achieve something you set up to do. To be realistic, your goal must represent an objective in which you are willing and able to work towards.While there is nothing wrong with shooting for the stars, it is important to investigate whether the goal is really applicable to you and your lifestyle.For the most effective goal setting, ensure you are planning your steps wisely and establishing a realistic time frame that will allow you to carry out those steps.
