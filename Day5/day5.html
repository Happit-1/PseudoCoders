DAY 5

Limit Your Social Media Usage.

Studies show that people who spend a significant amount of time on social media experience increased anxiety and decreased self-esteem.We may be sacrificing our mental health as well as our time by overusing social media, and inflicting needless stress on ourselves.Start spending time with your family instead. take a break from social media to clear your mind, and begin breaking your bad habit.There is nothing social about a platform when it has a more negative than positive effect on your health and well-being.
